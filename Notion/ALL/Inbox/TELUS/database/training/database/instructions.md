## **Instructions for Podcasts Top Results Qualification Test**

### Introduction:
After reading the guidelines of the evaluations, you are required to pass the test to be qualified to move on to live tasks. You are allowed three attempts per test. Make sure you read the instructions below before taking your first test.
- **Passing Scores:**Test 1: 90%

### **Begin Attempt**
- A notification will appear letting you know that the test has begun. Rate the result from the drop down menu. **Save Judgement** or **Save & Next** to move on to the next task.

### **Failed Attempt**
- If you failed a test, a yellow banner will notify you to try again. Click "Begin a New Test" to start a new attempt. **You will be disqualified after three failed attempts.**
