## **Task Overview**
**You will be given a user goal and specific prompt to start with and we ask you to build the voice conversation with each AI from there**, with follow up prompts as needed to achieve the goal to your satisfaction.
Please limit each conversation to between **3** and** 10 prompts** in order to maintain consistency across tasks.
After interacting with each AI, you will assess the overall quality of your experience with the product, and will be asked to provide an explanation for your quality assessment, with a focus on **how these products work for voice interaction**. In addition, you will be asked to respond to 8 additional dimensions of quality we are interested in, highlighting any issues that emerged within your interaction with the GenAI chatbots and judging the quality of more specific capabilities.
After interacting with both voice-based GenAI chatbots, you will be asked to rate which AI did better overall. When considering which interaction is better, think about how well each GenAI **acted as a good conversational partner in this spoken interaction.** By conversational partner, we mean that the AI chatbot should be **proactive, collaborative, and engaging** in how it works with you to help you navigate the situation or find solutions. A higher quality interaction is one in which the GenAI chatbot works with you to dive deeper into a topic or solve a nuanced problem.

## **Key Considerations**
When rating each experience, you should consider:
1. Are the GenAI responses appropriate for listening and engaging with it through voice?
1. Does the GenAI chatbot make it clear that it understands that you're looking to get help over the course of multiple turns?
1. Does the GenAI chatbot aid in refining your inquiry? Does it appropriately probe, seek details of your needs, or suggest potential next steps to ensure you get the output best suited to you, in a way that feels collaborative?
1. How relevant, informative, and focused are the GenAI chatbot's responses, in the context of the larger conversation?
1. How flexible is the GenAI chatbot in adapting to you and your needs as the course of the conversation progresses?
**Reminder:** Achieving your goal as fast as possible is not your objective for this task. Your objective is to evaluate your experience having a conversation with the GenAI chatbots. This means that shorter conversations are not inherently better than longer conversations.
